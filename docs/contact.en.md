# Contact

This page provides contact guidance for questions about IW3IP, teaching use, and development collaboration.

## Topics You Can Ask About

- questions about this site and the hands-on steps
- unclear points in the sample implementations or documentation
- workshop or classroom use
- collaborative development or joint research

## Contact Link

- Personal website: <https://www.ertl.jp/~yutaka/>

## What to Include in Your Message

When sending a question or consultation, the following information makes it easier to understand the situation.

- the page you were reading
- the steps or commands you ran
- the symptom or message you saw
- your environment
  - OS
  - versions of Docker, browser, Python, and related tools

## About Development Collaboration

If you are interested in development collaboration or joint research, please include the theme you are interested in and the work you are currently doing.  
Topics such as IoT data sharing, SSI / DID / VC, event sharing, and AI-based planning or control are particularly close to the material in this site.
