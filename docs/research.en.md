# Research

## Direction

The main focus is improving safety and security of embedded systems,
with practical relevance to domains such as automotive, rail, and aerospace.

## From standalone systems to connected systems

As IoT systems become networked and interdependent, research is conducted at two levels:

1. Assurance-oriented design for understandable standalone systems
2. Design and evaluation of large connected Systems of Systems (SoS)

## Bridging theory and practice

- Deployable software platform technologies
- Interdisciplinary expansion toward AI and socio-technical operation
- Modeling and assessment methods for large-scale connected systems
